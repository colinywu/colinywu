Hi, my name is Colin Wu and I am currently a first-year undergraduate student in the Computing Financial Management program at the University of Waterloo, set to graduate in 2024. I have many diverse interests which I aim to explore in my program. My passion for my future career can be best described by the intersection of finance and technology, captured by the rise of the fintech industry. Over the years, I have developed numerous technological skills to expand the breadth and depth of my abilities. As well, I have continued to build on my knowledge of key financial concepts, beginning my education in basic accounting courses and progressing into financial securities/investment. My main interests include programming, web design, investing/trading; interests reflected by my skills. Going into the future, I hope to build upon my past experiences with even more exciting ones in the professional world and hopefully contribute to society in a meaningful way.
